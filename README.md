# Daniel Diya

**Design engineer working on the canvas and in code.**

I started in frontend engineering and gradually moved closer to the product working on interface design, interaction, prototyping, and systems thinking.

Now I work across the full path from an idea to a shipped interface: figuring out how something should work, designing how it should feel, and building the final experience.

### Currently

- Exploring interaction, motion, and 3D as tools for making digital products feel more considered and physical

### I work with

**Design**  
Figma · Product design · Interaction design · Prototyping · Design systems

**Engineering**  
Next.js · TypeScript · React · Supabase · Motion.dev

**Exploring**  
Three.js · GSAP · Spline · Interactive visuals

### How I work

I am most interested in the space between design and engineering.

My work tends to be:

`simple enough to understand`  
`considered enough to feel intentional`  
`technical enough to survive implementation`
